Fried Rice

Description

Fried rice can be made with almost anything in your fridge or pantry (veggies,meat), and is super delicious

Simple fried rice can be seasoned with just soy sauce, or you can experiment with other sauces.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • veggies of choice (ex: onions, carrots, peas, green beans, broccoli, etc)
  • garlic and/or ginger
  • egg
  • protein of choice, if desired (chicken, beef, pork, spam, sausage, etc)
  • sauce or seasoning of choice (soy sauce, teriyaki, ketchup, gochujang, etc)

Steps

  1. Cook rice, let steam out to cool down and dry a bit
  2. Begin cooking protein, browning slightly
  3. When protein is almost cooked, add onions, cook until translucent
  4. Add veggies, hardest/densest first, cook until color begins to deepen
  5. Add rice, break apart larger chunks
  6. Scramble egg in seperate bowl, pour throughout pan
  7. As egg is cooking, add sauce, cook until desired thickness
